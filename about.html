Our story

Born from a love for slow baking.

AuroBakery started as a tiny home kitchen experiment in Bengaluru—supplying sourdough loaves and brownies to friends and neighbours. As word spread, what began as a weekend passion turned into a full-time bakehouse dedicated to honest, carefully crafted food.

Today, our team of pastry chefs and bakers combine classical French techniques with bold Indian flavours, working with local farms, sustainable dairies, and small producers across the country.

Crafted in small batches

We never freeze our doughs or rely on premixes. Each batch is made from scratch daily, following long fermentation times and precise techniques that respect the ingredients.

  • Stone-ground regional flours
  • Single-origin Indian cacao
  • Free-range eggs & organic dairy
  • No artificial colours in our core range

Inside the bakehouse

What “premium” means to us.

Ingredient-first

We prioritise traceable ingredients—Indian-origin cacao, real vanilla beans, French and European-style cultured butters, and seasonal fruits from trusted growers.

Technique-driven

From laminating dough for croissants to tempering chocolate and hand-piping decorations, our team is trained in precise patisserie methods.

Thoughtful design

Every cake and dessert is designed to balance flavour, texture, and aesthetics—minimal, elegant, and made to photograph beautifully without compromising taste.

The people

Meet the team behind the ovens.

Our core team brings experience from boutique patisseries, luxury hotels, and culinary schools around the world. We are united by a shared obsession: perfectly proofed dough, balanced flavours, and the magic of warm bread straight from the oven.

Arjun Mehta · Bread Lead

The Auro Crew

  • We’re constantly developing vegan and gluten-conscious options.
